How does it work?

Similar to how popular messaging boards allow you to create groups with friends, BORD allows you to create groups where you can quickly post pending payments.

BORD's system is extremely simple and intuitive.

Through the use of a clean and sleek UI in addition to a streamlined payment process, BORD's superior payment method enables payments to fit into your life, not the other way around.

BORD combines the concept of a bulletin board in order to create a sense of community with your peers, while also using a visually appealing payment system.

Why use BORD?

While finally arriving to the stage where groups must pay one another, the situation can be really tricky when trying to document and remember every single purchase and for the exact amount.
BORD combines a board where you can post all your purchases and ties it with our extremely simple payment method. This allows for clear communication between devices and people, while minimizing the reliance on memory and organization.
