How to use BORD

Creating

By navigating to the create page of this dashboard, you can create a BORD and share it with users and friends that also have the app.

Adding

After you've created a BORD and shared it with the proper peers and friends, you can begin to add payments and transactions by inputting each of the pieces of information in their respective slot.
